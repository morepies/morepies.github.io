Refund Policy

Updated: 2026-02-07

1. Scope

This policy applies to digital services and technical support, including setup guidance, script adjustments, and customization assistance.

2. Refundable Cases

  • Service has not started and cancellation is requested within 24 hours after payment.
  • Confirmed inability to deliver agreed scope and both parties agree to terminate.
  • Duplicate charge or clear billing error.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Service was delivered according to confirmed scope and already accepted/used.
  • Outcome differences caused by third-party platform policy changes, account limits, or network conditions.
  • Issues introduced by your own environment/code modifications.

4. How to Request

Please submit order number, issue description, and evidence screenshots within 7 days after payment to: morepiesai@gmail.com

5. Processing Time

Reviews usually complete in 3-7 business days. Refunds are returned via original payment channel, subject to payment platform timing.
